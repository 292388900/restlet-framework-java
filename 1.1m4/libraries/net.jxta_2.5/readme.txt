JXTA Technology
---------------

"JXTA technology is a set of open protocols that allow any connected device on 
the network ranging from cell phones and wireless PDAs to PCs and servers to 
communicate and collaborate in a P2P manner. JXTA is the industry-leading P2P 
technology, supported by over 30,000 members worldwide with downloads exceeding 
12 Million."
