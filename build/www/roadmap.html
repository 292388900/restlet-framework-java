Roadmap

Goal

Our immediate goal is to release a standardized version 1.0 of the Restlet API and at the same time a complete and stable version of the NRE. We will also provide professional support for this version via Noelios Consulting.

Timeline

  • 2007 Q1
    • [Optional] 1.0 RC2 release
    • 1.0 release. List of issues must be resolved (documentation, etc.).
  • 2007 Q2
    • 1.1 beta 1.Open issues to resolve.
  • 2007 Q3
    • 1.1 release. Remaining issues must be resolved.
  • 2008 Q1
    • [JSR] Submission of a proposal to standardize a Restlet API 2.0 to the JCP
    • [JSR] If submission approved, formation of expert group
    • [JSR] Write first draft
  • 2008 Q2
    • [JSR] Updates to the draft (optional)
    • [JSR] Public Review
  • 2008 Q3
    • [JSR] Drafts updates (optional)
    • [JSR] Draft Specification approval ballot by EC
  • 2008 Q4
    • [JSR] Proposed final draft
    • [JSR] Completion of RI and TCK
  • 2009 Q1
    • [JSR] Final draft Submission
    • [JSR] Final approval ballot by EC
    • [JSR] Final release of Restlet API 2.0
    • 2.0 release

Conclusion

Due to the open source nature of the Restlet project, the above timeline is subject to change. However, we will do our best to respect it and update it as frequently as possible. Please, feel free to contact us for questions and comments.
